About Us

At Color Master, we excel in crafting natural solutions for food, pharma, and cosmetics. Our commitment
to purity and innovation drives us to create exceptional products that enhance well-being and
beauty, all while respecting the environment. Discover nature's finest with Color Master.

History

In 1988, Color Master blossomed under Dr. Sarah Evans' vision. By 1993, eco-extraction techniques
marked a turning point. The 90s saw Color Master
collaborate with pharmaceutical giants.

The new millennium, 2003, brought global expansion—research hubs in Asia and Europe.
Color Master continued to flourish, launching organic cosmetics in 2008
championing clean beauty.

By 2013, sustainability became the core—carbon neutrality achieved. In 2018,
plant-based colorants revolutionized the industry.

Now in 2023, Color Master celebrates 35 years as an icon of natural color innovation,
serving global industries with brilliance.
